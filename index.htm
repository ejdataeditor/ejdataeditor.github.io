Welcome to the Economic Journal's Data Editor website!
 

This website provides all the relevant information regarding the Data Policy and pre-acceptance reproducibility checks implemented at the Economic Journal. In the different sections of this website you will find the following information:

  • Before you submit:
    • Read here about our Data Policy and make sure you comply with all requirements before you submit the paper for the first time. Check the cases in which exemptions to the policy can be granted and the procedures to obtain them. If you don't request the exemption at the time of first submission, you will not be able to do it later
  • Preparing the package:
    • Learn how to prepare and submit your replication package. Find here some additional advice on guidelines and best practices for reproducible research.
  • Replicate:
    • Looking for reproducible papers to replicate? Find here indications on where to find and how to use replication packages of papers published in the Economic Journal.
  • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):
    • Find a long list of answers to frequently asked questions that can clarify some queries you may have.
  • Data Editor:
    • Find more about our Data Editor, including contact information.
