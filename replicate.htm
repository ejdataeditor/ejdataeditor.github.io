Replicate papers published at the Economic Journal

Looking for reproducible papers to replicate? The Economic Journal started publishing the replication packages along with the papers on the Journal's website a long time ago. Starting December 2019, when the first Data Editor was appointed, these packages went through reproducibility checks. Starting April 2021, we collected all replication packages at the Economic Journal's community of Zenodo:
