The Data Editor of the Economic Journal defines and monitors the Economic Journal's approach to data and reproducibility. In particular, s/he is the responsible of overlooking the correct implementation of the Journal's Data Policy for all articles, and of coordinating the team of replicators that conduct reproducibility checks on all articles that are conditionally accepted for publication at the Economic Journal.

 

The current Data Editor of the Economic Journal is Joan Llull, who is is Director of MOVE, Associate Professor of Economics at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, and Associate Research Professor at the Barcelona GSE. He has served as board member of the Review of Economic Studies, as associate editor of SERIEs - The Journal of the Spanish Economic Association, and as guest editor of Labour Economics. He has been Data Editor of the Economic Journal since December 2019. You can contact him at ejdataeditor@gmail.com.
